About LeafLogic

We're revolutionizing indoor gardening through cutting-edge AI technology and robotics, making plant care accessible, efficient, and sustainable for everyone.

Our Mission

To democratize indoor gardening by creating intelligent, autonomous systems that make plant care effortless and enjoyable. We believe everyone should have access to the benefits of living with plants, regardless of their experience level.

Our AI-powered robots monitor, water, and care for your plants 24/7, ensuring optimal growth conditions while you focus on enjoying the beauty and benefits of your indoor garden.
