About Botani

Botani represents the future of indoor gardening. Our AI-powered robot combines cutting-edge technology with nature's wisdom to create the perfect environment for your plants to flourish.

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy and benefits of thriving plants, regardless of their gardening experience. Botani makes plant care effortless, intelligent, and accessible to all, while promoting a deeper connection with nature in our modern lives.
